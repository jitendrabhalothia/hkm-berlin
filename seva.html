The Meaning of Seva

Why Sponsoring Seva is Special

❀

In Bhakti Yoga, seva means devotional service — any act performed as an offering to the Lord. Sponsoring the chariot, the prasadam or the fruits is not merely a donation. It is a direct act of devotion.

"If one offers Me with love and devotion a leaf, a flower, fruit or water, I will accept it."
— Bhagavad-Gita 9.26

Srila Prabhupada taught that sponsoring Ratha Yatra is among the most auspicious acts. Lord Jagannath personally blesses those who assist in His festival — in this life and beyond.
