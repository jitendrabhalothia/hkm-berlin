Take the Holy Name to the Streets

Hare Krishna Hare Krishna · Krishna Krishna Hare Hare

Hare Rama Hare Rama · Rama Rama Hare Hare

We walk through Berlin's streets chanting the Maha Mantra with mridanga, harmonium and kartals — sharing peace and joy with everyone we meet. Join us! 🙏

Register to Join
🥁

What We Do

Walk through Berlin chanting the Maha Mantra together — with drums, harmonium and hand cymbals. No experience needed.

⏱️

Duration & Details

1–2 hours of walking and chanting. We end with free prasadam (vegetarian snacks). We will check availability and share the meeting point with you.

🌍

Open to Everyone

All backgrounds, all religions, all ages. Just come with an open heart. 100% free — always.

"Simply by chanting the holy name of the Lord, one is liberated. There is no rule or regulation, no qualification or disqualification."

— Śrīla Prabhupāda
