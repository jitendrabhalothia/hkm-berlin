What is Harinam?

Sankirtan — The Yoga of Sound

❀
🥁

What We Do

We walk through Berlin's streets chanting the Hare Krishna Maha Mantra with mridanga drums, kartals (hand cymbals) and harmonium — spreading peace and joy to everyone we meet.

📖

What the Scriptures Say

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu taught that Harinam Sankirtan is the yuga-dharma — the spiritual practice for this age. Simply hearing the Holy Name purifies the heart.

🌍

Open to Everyone

No prior experience needed. No special clothes. Just come with an open heart. We welcome people of all religions, backgrounds and ages — even if you've never chanted before.

🎁

Completely Free

Harinam is a gift to the city. No entry fee, no donations required, no membership. We also distribute free prasadam (sacred vegetarian snacks) during the walk.

"In this age of Kali, the holy name of the Lord, the Hare Krishna Maha Mantra, is the incarnation of God. Simply by chanting the holy name, one associates directly with God and is liberated."

— Śrīla Prabhupāda, Caitanya-caritāmṛta
How It Works

What to Expect

❀
1
Register below — pick your preferred date. We'll confirm via WhatsApp or email with the exact meeting point and time.
2
Meet the group — we gather at the meeting point, do a short introduction, distribute instruments (kartals) if you want to play.
3
Walk & chant — we walk for 1–2 hours through Berlin streets chanting together. Pace is gentle, stops for photos and prasadam distribution.
4
Prasadam & community — we end together with free prasadam (vegetarian snacks) and time to connect with fellow devotees.
