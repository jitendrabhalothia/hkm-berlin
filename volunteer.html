Volunteer with Us

Serve Lord Jagannath and be part of something extraordinary.
Join 1000+ devotees for the Festival of the Chariots in Berlin.

18 July 2026 10:00 AM onwards Sri Ganesha Hindu Temple, Hasenheide 106, Berlin
How You Can Serve

Volunteer Roles

❀

Kirtan

Singing the holy names and playing instruments during the procession and program.

Prasadam Serving

Distributing free vegan & vegetarian prasadam to 1000+ devotees and guests.

Cooking

Preparing sacred prasadam in the kitchen with love and devotion.

Deity Service

Taking care of Lord Jagannath, Baladeva and Subhadra — altar setup and service.

Chariot Making

Building and decorating the beautiful Ratha Yatra chariot.

Security

Managing crowd flow and ensuring a safe and joyful experience for all.

Assisting VIPs

Welcoming and guiding special guests with warmth and care.

Photography / Video

Capturing the divine festival for memories and social media.

Setup & Cleanup

Pre-event setup and post-event cleanup — essential service.
