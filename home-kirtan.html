What We Bring to Your Home

❀

By the grace of Śrīla Prabhupāda, we humbly come to your home and share the joy of Kirtan with your family and guests. 🙏

Hare Krishna Kirtan

Live call-and-response chanting of the Maha Mantra — mridanga drum, harmonium and kartals. Uplifting, joyful and deeply meditative.

Mantra Meditation

Guided japa meditation on the Hare Krishna Maha Mantra — calming for the mind, purifying for the heart. Perfect for beginners.

Spiritual Talk

Practical wisdom from the Bhagavad-Gita on happiness, purpose, relationships and inner peace — inspiring and accessible for all.

Family-Friendly Gathering

A warm, welcoming satsang for the whole family. Children love the music. Elders appreciate the prayers. Everyone feels the peace.

What the Scriptures Say

The Ancient Authority of Sankirtan

❀

"In this age, intelligent persons perform saṅkīrtana-yajña — the congregational chanting of the Lord's holy names."

— Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam 11.5.32

"Harer nāma harer nāma harer nāma eva kevalam" — Only the chanting of the Holy Name, only the Holy Name, only the Holy Name.

— Bṛhan-nāradīya Purāṇa

"Chanting the holy name cleanses the heart of all impurities and brings transcendental joy beyond all material happiness."

— Śikṣāṣṭaka 1, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu
