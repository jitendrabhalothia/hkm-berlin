Lord Jagannath's chariot rolls through Berlin on 18 July 2026.
Register your attendance — free for everyone. All are welcome.

What to Expect

A Day of Joy & Devotion

❀

Chariot Procession

Pull Lord Jagannath's magnificent chariot through the streets of Berlin. A rare and sacred experience.

Kirtan & Music

Live kirtan, devotional music and the joyful chanting of Hare Krishna Maha Mantra throughout the day.

Free Prasadam

Free vegan & vegetarian sacred prasadam for 600+ guests. Prepared with love and devotion.

Spiritual Programs

Bhagavad-Gita talks, mantra meditation, and blessings from Lord Jagannath, Baladeva and Subhadra.

600+ Devotees

Join hundreds of devotees and guests from across Berlin and beyond for this divine celebration.

Free Entry

Absolutely free. No tickets, no registration fee. Everyone is welcome — families, children, friends.
