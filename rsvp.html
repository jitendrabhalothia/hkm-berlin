Narsimha Yagna

🔥

The day begins with a sacred Narsimha Yagna — a Vedic fire ceremony invoking the protection and blessings of Lord Narasimha (the lion-man incarnation of Lord Vishnu). In the presence of the Supreme Lord, there can be no possibility of any danger.

NRSIMHA KAVACA BIJA MANTRA

ugram vīram mahā-viṣṇum
jvalantaṁ sarvato mukham
nṛsiṁhaṁ bhīṣaṇaṁ bhadram
mṛtyur mṛtyuṁ namāmy aham

Translation:

"I bow down to Lord Narasimha who is fierce and heroic like Lord Mahavishnu. He is burning from every side. He is terrible, auspicious and the death of death itself."

Mantra Name: Ugram Viram Mahavishnum · Nrsimha Kavaca Bija Mantra

"In the presence of the sun, there can be no question of darkness. Similarly, in the presence of the Supreme Lord, there can be no possibility of any danger. When one takes shelter of Lord Narasimha who is known as the Death of Death-personified, he becomes completely fearless."

— From the glory of Lord Nrsimhadeva

Listen to the Ugram Viram Mahavishnum mantra — chant along with us:

Transform fearfulness to fearlessness — the ultimate chant to overcome all danger

Bhakta Prahlada was able to overcome all kinds of obstacles and dangers simply by the grace of Lord Narasimha. Join us at 9:45 AM on 18 July as we invoke His protection before the Ratha Yatra begins. Register below — the same form covers both events.
