🔥 Sacred Vedic Fire Ceremony — Berlin — Free & Open to All

Śrī Nṛsiṁha Havan

Invoking the protection and blessings of Lord Narasimha
before the grand Ratha Yātrā Berlin 2026

Saturday, 18 July 2026 9:45 AM – 10:15 AM Sri Ganesha Hindu Temple, Hasenheide 106, Berlin
Register for the Havan

NṚSIṀHA KAVACA BĪJA MANTRA

ugram vīram mahā-viṣṇum
jvalantaṁ sarvato mukham
nṛsiṁhaṁ bhīṣaṇaṁ bhadram
mṛtyur mṛtyuṁ namāmy aham

"I bow down to Lord Narasimha — fierce, heroic, blazing on all sides — the death of death itself."

Ugram Vīram Mahāviṣṇum · Nṛsiṁha Kavaca Bīja Mantra

Chant along — transform fearfulness to fearlessness

9:45 AM
🔥 Śrī Nṛsiṁha Havan
