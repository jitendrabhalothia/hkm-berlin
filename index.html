Ratha Yatra Quiz 🎡

How much do you know about the Festival of the Chariots?

Question 1 of 4

What does the name "Jagannath" mean in Sanskrit?

🌍 "Jagat" means universe and "natha" means Lord. Srila Prabhupada wrote: "Jagannath is the Lord of everyone — He belongs to all castes, all religions, all nations."

In which Indian city did Ratha Yatra originate over 5,000 years ago?

🏙️ The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha is one of the four sacred dhamas (holy places) mentioned in the Vedic scriptures. Srila Prabhupada visited Puri personally and was deeply inspired by Lord Jagannath.

Srila Prabhupada organised the first Ratha Yatra outside India in which Western city?

🌉 In 1967, Srila Prabhupada organised the first ever Ratha Yatra outside India on the streets of San Francisco. It was a historic moment — tens of thousands of people joined. Today Ratha Yatra is celebrated in hundreds of cities worldwide!

How many chariots are pulled in the traditional Ratha Yatra of Puri?

🎪 Three chariots carry Lord Jagannath, His brother Lord Baladeva, and His sister Subhadra Devi. Lord Jagannath's chariot is called Nandighosa, with 16 wheels and decorated in yellow and red cloth.

What We Practice

Four pillars of Bhakti Yoga at our Berlin community — all are welcome, free of charge

Kirtan

Congregational chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra every Saturday at 4 PM.

Bhagavad-Gita

Weekly study of the Gita As It Is — Srila Prabhupada's exact commentary.

Meditation

Mantra meditation on japa beads — peaceful practice for body and mind.

Free Prasadam

Sanctified vegetarian food shared every Saturday at 6 PM. Everyone welcome.
