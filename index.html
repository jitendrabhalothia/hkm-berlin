What We Practice

Four pillars of Bhakti Yoga at our Berlin community — all are welcome, free of charge

Kirtan

Congregational chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra every Saturday at 4 PM.

Bhagavad-Gita

Weekly study of the Gita As It Is — Srila Prabhupada's exact commentary.

Meditation

Mantra meditation on japa beads — peaceful practice for body and mind.

Free Prasadam

Sanctified vegetarian food shared every Saturday at 6 PM. Everyone welcome.

Our Spiritual Master

The Hare Krishna Movement Berlin follows His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada as our eternal Diksha Guru and Founder-Acharya. Everything we teach and practice is directly based on his books, lectures, and instructions.

Come as you are — no membership, no fees, no prior knowledge needed. Just an open heart and a willingness to chant.
