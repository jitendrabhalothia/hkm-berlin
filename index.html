Welcome to Berlin

What is Bhakti Yoga?

❀

Bhakti Yoga is the ancient path of devotional service — the yoga of love and surrender to the Supreme. Rooted in the Vedic scriptures and brought to the Western world by Srila Prabhupada in 1965, Bhakti Yoga offers a practical, joyful approach to self-realisation that anyone can follow.

At the Hare Krishna Movement Berlin, we practice Bhakti Yoga through kirtan (congregational chanting), Bhagavad-Gita study, prasadam (sanctified vegetarian food), and devotional community. Everyone is welcome — regardless of background, faith, or experience.

Paths of Devotion

❀

Four pillars of Bhakti Yoga practice at our Berlin community

Kirtan

Congregational chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra — pure joy for the heart and soul.

Bhagavad-Gita

Weekly study of the Bhagavad-Gita As It Is, guided by Srila Prabhupada's commentary.

Meditation

Mantra meditation on japa beads — a personal, peaceful daily practice for everyone.

Prasadam

Free vegetarian food offered to Krishna — nourishing for body, mind and spirit.

The Hare Krishna Maha-Mantra

Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare

The supreme method of self-realisation in this age — chanting the holy names of God cleanses the heart and brings one to the platform of pure love.

New to Bhakti Yoga?

Everyone Is Welcome

❀

Whether you are completely new to spirituality or have been practising for years, the Hare Krishna community in Berlin welcomes you with open arms. There is no requirement to become a member, change your lifestyle overnight, or hold any prior belief.

Simply come, chant, listen, and taste the sweet prasadam. Srila Prabhupada said: "Don't try to understand God — just chant His name and He will reveal Himself to you."
