Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Hare Krishna Berlin and Bhakti Yoga Germany

About Us

What is Hare Krishna Berlin?

Hare Krishna Berlin is a devotional community in Germany following the teachings of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada. We practise Bhakti Yoga — the yoga of devotion — through kirtan (mantra chanting), Bhagavad-Gita study and free prasadam (sacred food) every Saturday in Berlin-Wedding.

Do I need to be Hindu to attend?

Absolutely not. People of all religions, nationalities and backgrounds attend our programs. Bhakti Yoga is a universal spiritual path. As Srila Prabhupada said: "Krishna consciousness is not a sectarian religion — it is the eternal function of the soul."

Is there a membership fee or registration?

No — everything is completely free. No registration, no membership, no experience required. Come as you are. We welcome everyone with open arms.

Weekly Programs

Where are the weekly programs held in Berlin?

Our weekly programs are held every Saturday in Berlin-Wedding, 5 minutes walk from Osloer Str. (U9/U8). Join our WhatsApp community for the exact address each week — we share it every Saturday morning.

What is the schedule on Saturday?

4:00 PM — Kirtan (devotional mantra chanting with instruments)
4:30 PM — Bhagavad-Gita class (open to all levels)
5:30 PM — Q&A discussion
6:00 PM — Free vegetarian prasadam (sanctified food)

What should I wear?

Come as you are — there is no dress code. Comfortable, modest clothing is appreciated. Please remove your shoes before entering the program room.

Can children come?

Yes! Children are very welcome. Programs are family-friendly. Children love the live music, the chanting and especially the free prasadam!

Bhakti Yoga & The Mantra

What is Bhakti Yoga?

Bhakti Yoga is the spiritual path of devotion to God (Krishna). It includes chanting the Hare Krishna mantra, studying the Bhagavad-Gita, and serving others with love and devotion. It is described in Vedic scriptures as the most powerful path for spiritual realisation in this age.

What is the Hare Krishna Maha Mantra?

The Hare Krishna Maha Mantra is:

Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare
Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare

These 16 words are a direct prayer to the Lord and His energy. Chanting this mantra purifies the mind and connects us with God through transcendental sound vibration. It is free, requires no initiation and is open to absolutely everyone.

What is kirtan?

Kirtan is the congregational chanting of God's names with music — mridanga drums, kartals (hand cymbals) and harmonium. A lead singer sings a phrase and everyone repeats it. It is deeply meditative, joyful and open to all — no musical experience needed.

Ratha Yatra Berlin 2026

What is Ratha Yatra?

Ratha Yatra — the Festival of the Chariots — is one of the world's oldest festivals, originating in Puri, India over 5,000 years ago. Lord Jagannath (the Lord of the Universe) comes out of His temple to bless all people equally. Anyone who pulls the rope of Lord Jagannath's chariot receives immense spiritual blessings.

When and where is Ratha Yatra Berlin 2026?

Date: Saturday, 18 July 2026
Time: 10:00 AM onwards
Location: Sri Ganesha Hindu Temple, Hasenheide 106, 10967 Berlin
Entry: Free — no ticket needed
Prasadam: Free vegetarian food for 600+ guests

How do I register for Ratha Yatra?

Registration is not required to attend — just come! However if you would like to let us know you are coming (so we can prepare enough prasadam), please fill in the RSVP form on our website. It takes 30 seconds and helps us plan for everyone.
